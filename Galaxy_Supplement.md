## B17. Experimental Limitations (Honest Record)

1. **Self-evaluation bias** (AI experiments): gpt-4o generated and evaluated its own outputs. Ceiling effect observed (most scores converge to 9.0 after parameter release).

2. **Literature-based data** (Cell cycle, HRV): Based on published averages, not original measurements. Confirms direction but not independent.

3. **Sample sizes**: DNA Phase 1 (N=12), Language (N=10), Cell cycle (N=10). Statistical power is limited.

4. **Post-hoc reinterpretations**: A2 (vowel asymmetry) and C3 (volume) results were reinterpreted after initial prediction failed. These are clearly labeled and should not be counted as confirmations.

5. **Single prompt** (AI experiment): Only one prompt tested. Generalization requires multiple domains.

6. **System Prompt design flaw** (AI experiment): "Physics only, 50 words" conflicted fundamentally with the test prompt. CTL dominance may reflect experimental design, not universal Re structure.
